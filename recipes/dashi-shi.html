Odin's Recipes Dashi-shi

A picture of Dashi-shi, a local Northern Nigerian cuisine

INGREDIENTS

  • Wheat
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Piece Green beans
  • Cucumber
  • Spices
  • Veg Oil
  • tomatoes, Sweet pepper, Red pepper

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Grind ur wheat wash it and put it in steamer to steam for like 1 hour
  2. Cut in ur tomatoes,sweet nd red paper then grind
  3. Then u Take in ur clean pot and add ur vegetables oil and onions for frying then you pour the veggies
  4. Then u take ur cabbage,carrot,green beans nd piece and then put them at the last
